About Us

At Numbers Library, we strongly uphold the reading culture. Numbers-Book Library is a platform where students who like reading pick different books from a range of topics. Access to this library is only available to EDU students, EDU LF's and a EDU staff members. Get started by presenting your library ID and signing up. Users have up to 3 days to return books borrowed.
